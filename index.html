About us

Sometimes people encounter problems that are beyond them, and then it sinks in ― no one really has it all. The next step is to ask for support and that may be the game-changer in the scenario.

It all begins with the realization that changes are made one step at a time, a little help here and a little help there is all that is needed to get going. Owambe is place where you can raise requests for financial support ― and also reach out to people who need financial help.

No one said the road would be easy but you can definitely start from here.
Owambe is committed to being a voice that rings out loud for everyone to hear. There is value in connecting with others and it shouldn't be overlooked. So if you will like to assist people who need it or you want your cry for help to be heard, welcome aboard.

How it works

We make a thorough investigation to separate those who want assistance from scammers. So any individual or organisation listed here has been screened and found genuine.

To receive help, you have to be plain about who you are and why you want it; people tend to be more generous when they know the full details. That means you have to undergo a background check.
If your request is approved, you will be listed on Owambe and your profile will be made public so you can receive donations. We will send your funds to you via Paypal, bank wire or cryptocurrency.

In addition, you will update us on what you were able to do with the money. Your update will be made public so donors can get feedback on how their donations was spent.
